# Value Investing — Reference Material

## Conservative Defaults

| Parameter | Default | Max allowed |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| Growth Yr 1–5 | 5% | 12% |
| Growth Yr 6–10 | Growth1 / 2 | — |
| Discount rate | 10% | — |
| Terminal multiple | 12x | 15x |
| Margin of safety threshold | 50% | — |

## Worked Example: Condensed Dhandho Analysis

**Given:** A consumer staples company with:
- Current FCF: $500M, Shares: 200M, Price: $35
- 5yr avg ROE: 18%, D/E: 0.3, P/E: 11x, FCF Yield: 7.2%
- FCF CAGR (5yr): 6.5%, Forward EPS consensus: stable

**Stage 1 — Screen:**
P/E 11x < 15 ✅ | FCF Yield 7.2% > 6% ✅ | ROE 18% > 15% ✅ | D/E 0.3 < 0.5 ✅
→ 4/4 flags pass. Proceed.

**Stage 3 — DCF:**
Growth 1-5: 6.5% (historical CAGR) | Growth 6-10: 3.25% | Discount: 10% | Multiple: 12x
→ IV = $42.15/share | Price = $35 | Margin of Safety = 17%

**Verdict:** Quantitative pass, but margin of safety (17%) below 50% threshold.
Action: Watchlist — wait for price decline to ~$21 for adequate margin, or investigate further.
